

















The History
of every major
Galactic
Civilization tends to pass
through three distinct and recognizable phases,
those of Survival, Inquiry
and Sophistication, otherwise known as
the How, Why, and Where phases. For instance,
the
first
phase
is characterized by the question 'How can we eat?' the second by the question 'Why do we eat?' and the third by the question 'Where shall we have lunch?
